Week 1

Progress Made

During this week we went over the idea of what exactly an attic ventilation system is and why it is important for regulating temperatures inside a home. Marlon, who is the person who came up with the idea explained it very well and we became more convinced that it could be a sucessful product if actually executed correctly. The idea is that we have a smart attic ventilation system which uses a PCB to control the attic fans and the PCB also has internet and bluetooth capabilties so that we can have a web app that controls the attic fan through the PCB.

  • Researched what an attic ventilation system is
  • Discussed problems that we might have with existing business model and implementation issues
  • Marlon cleared any doubts we might have had with the idea as his idea was very flushed out and many broad details were covered well

Issues Faced and Challenges

There were some issues that we had initially with this idea, specifically the feasibility of the ideas Marlon presented such as the idea that we are relying on working with existing companies already established in the attic vent space. We were thinking that these ideas presented were more features than a full product if anything as we are simply adding onto an exsting product. But we thought about it more and figured that our idea can be a full featured product if we make it a hardware and software integration to existing attic fans. In this way, we've added something that is more than a feature but a reinvention of how attic fans should operate.

  • Feasibility and practicality issues of our product
  • Distinguishing our idea from a feature or a product
  • Some doubts we had whether the idea was a compelling idea that other ventilation companies would invest in themselves, we thought about this idea was probably already thought about by companies adding may have been overlooked for whatever reason such as expenses.
