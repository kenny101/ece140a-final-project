Week 2

Progress Made

During this week we began brainstorming and researching the feasibility of our product through statistical research and conducting analysis through tools we learned in class such as SWOT analysis which analyzes what other companies have to offer and what opportunies can be had to incorporate into our product. We also began interviewing people we knew that fit this product niche. In this case, our product niche were general homeowners. We interviewed them by asking them their knowledge of attic vents as it was important that our customers would be knowledgable enough about attic fans in order for them to be convinced that they needed our product.

  • Researched attic ventilation system
  • Conducted analysis on our product to determine practicality through SWOT analysis and other methods taught in class such as user stories
  • Interviewed our customer product niche to determine practicality and desirability of features of out product or whether or not anything additional needed to be added to make our product better or more profitable.

This was important as the cost of the materials, labor, and other cost logistics has a significant weigh on the success of our product.
