Week 3

Progress Made

This was the final week of our report and we had figured out many logistics such as competition, target market, problems to be solved, etc. We looked into our competition and came up with two companies that were very well established in this market which were QuietCool and AirVent. QuietCool was a company which was already integrating IoT into their devices which was an issue with this as our product aimed to solve similar problems which QuietCool aimed to solve which was to make attic ventilation more efficient by saving energy, thereby saving costs and lowering carbon emissions. However, their product lacked something that we thought was really important which was that it did not take into account forecast and other external factors required to operate efficiently. Users would have to manually set threshhold values to turn on/off the attic vent if they wanted it to operate more efficiently. We came up with an idea to have our product be plug and play so that the user does not need to worry about their attic vents operating or relying on their knowledge to set threshhold values for the attic vents.
